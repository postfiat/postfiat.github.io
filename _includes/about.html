About Post Fiat

Post Fiat Capital is one of the first institutionally focused crypto asset managers.

Investors want to participate in the cryptocurrency revolution without outsized monthly volatility and drawdowns and recognize the benefit of active management in a space with a diversity of project viability, management execution capability and investor incentive alignment.

Post Fiat achieves more consistent returns by hedging long positions with futures and derivatives. We aim to deliver attractive risk-adjusted returns with minimal relative volatility and competitive absolute returns vis-a-vis other asset classes.

Post Fiat's founders have managed $1+ billion TMT portfolios at the world's largest hedge funds. They've started series A tech start ups, worked at Palantir, Goldman Sachs, Merrill Lynch, and Citi FX.
